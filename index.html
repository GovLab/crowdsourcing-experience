The Questionnaire

The Crowdsourcing Design Advisor is designed to help decision-makers in the public and private sector make an informed decision about when and how to use crowdsourcing.

Because of your experience with crowdsourcing, we would like to ask you for your help.

Thank you in advance!

By answering this questionnaire you will give us enough information to:

  1. Create and add your case-study in our free, downloadable and open crowdsourcing knowledge base;
  2. Leverage and input your learnings and experience into the Advisor, and;
  3. Help advance the research on identifying the best practices on crowdsourcing.
